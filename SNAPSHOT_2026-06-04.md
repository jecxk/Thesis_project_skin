## 7. Critical implementation details (do not regress)

**Grad-CAM target layers:**

| Model | Target layer |
|---|---|
| EfficientNet-B0 | `model.blocks[-1][-1]` |
| ResNet50 | `model.layer4[-1]` |
| DenseNet121 | `model.features.norm5` |
| Swin-Tiny | `model.layers[-1].blocks[-1].norm2` |

For EfficientNet, never revert to `model.bn2` — SiLU after BN zeroes ~50 % of
activations and produces a flat blue heatmap. The fix is in commit `2ceaa3c`.
